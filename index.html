About Bonanza: Season 2

Bonanza: Season 2 is the second edition of the most anticipated basketball tournament at Dr. B.C. Roy Engineering College. This season promises to be bigger and better with more teams, intense competition, and thrilling matches. Open to students from all courses and years, this tournament provides a platform for athletes to showcase their skills and passion for basketball. Don’t miss your chance to be part of the action!
